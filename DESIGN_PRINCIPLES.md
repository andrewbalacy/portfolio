# Design Principles

## 1.
Premium over flashy.

---

## 2.
Whitespace is a feature.

---

## 3.
Motion communicates hierarchy.

---

## 4.
Every section must tell part of the story.

---

## 5.
Build curiosity before explanation.

---

## 6.
Healthcare influences the mission.
Software expresses the solution.

---

## 7.
If a feature doesn't strengthen the story, remove it.

---

## 8.
Make visitors feel like they're using a product—not reading a résumé.

---

## 9.
Everything should feel intentional.

---

## 10.
Craftsmanship is the product.
